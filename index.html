Letters to the Stars

You are the space between stars —
vast, silent, and full of wonder.
Where others see emptiness, I see you:
infinite depth, infinite possibility,
infinite home.

— Written for you, always

Even the moon, in all her silver glory,
envies how brightly you shine.
She borrows light from distant suns —
but you? You are the source itself.
You never borrowed. You just are.

— For my eternal light

My love for you has no edge —
like the universe, it only ever expands.
Scientists say space grows faster than light can cross it.
That is how fast I fall for you
every single morning.

— Expanding, always expanding

If I were made of stardust —
and I am, and so are you —
then loving you is the universe
recognizing itself
in the most beautiful mirror ever made.

— Star to star, always

There are 8 billion people on this earth.
In another life, another body, another time —
I would wander through every single one
until I found the warmth of your laugh
and I would know. Still. Always. You.

— Across every universe
